Rule method_chaining_indentation¶

Method chaining MUST be properly indented. Method chaining with different levels of indentation is not supported.

Examples¶

Example #1¶

--- Original
+++ New
 <?php
 $user->setEmail('voff.web@gmail.com')
-         ->setPassword('233434');
+    ->setPassword('233434');
